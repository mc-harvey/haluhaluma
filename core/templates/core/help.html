Frequently Asked Questions

We're here to help! Our comprehensive FAQ section is designed to provide quick answers to your most common questions about the marketplace, from account management to product listings and more.

How do I create an account? How can I post a product for sale? How do I contact a seller? What payment methods are supported?

How do I create an account?

To create an account, click the "Sign Up" button in the top-right corner and fill out the registration form with your details.

How can I post a product for sale?

After logging in, go to the "Products" page and click the "Add Product" button. You will be prompted to enter details about your item and upload an image.

How do I contact a seller?

On any product's detail page, you can find a "Message" button to send a private message directly to the seller.

What payment methods are supported?

We do not handle payments directly. All transactions and payment methods are arranged between the buyer and the seller.
